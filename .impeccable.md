## Design Context

### Users
**Primary audiences:**
1. **Property buyers** — Searching for apartments/houses, comparing options, need trust signals and clear information to make a major financial decision.
2. **Property renters** — Looking for rental options, value speed and clarity, often browsing on mobile.
3. **Property owners** — Want to list their property for sale or rental, need to feel the platform is professional and will represent their asset well.

**Context of use:** Browsing from home or commute (mobile-heavy), often comparing multiple options across tabs. Decisions are high-stakes and emotional — buying/renting a home is one of the biggest life decisions. Users need to feel safe, informed, and guided.

**Job to be done:** Find the right property quickly with confidence, or trust this brokerage to sell/rent their property effectively.

### Brand Personality
**Three words:** Cercana, efectiva, impecable.

**Voice & tone:** Professional but approachable. Not cold corporate — warm professionalism. The kind of real estate agent you'd recommend to a friend: sharp, knowledgeable, honest, and responsive. Speaks clearly without jargon.

**Emotional goals:** Confianza, seriedad, sofisticación, modernidad, honestidad, profesionalismo, agilidad, oportunidad.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone:** Refined, modern luxury that feels accessible — not intimidating wealth, but sophisticated taste. Think: a well-designed boutique hotel lobby, not a marble-clad corporate tower. Clean lines, generous whitespace, confident typography. Every detail communicates "we care about quality."

**Theme:** Light mode. The interface should feel open, airy, and inviting — like walking into a well-lit, beautifully staged home.

**Anti-references:**
- NOT a massive portal like Portalinmobiliario.com — avoid dense grids, crowded UI, feature overload, banner ads aesthetic
- NOT generic Bootstrap real estate templates — avoid sameness
- NOT dark/moody/startup aesthetic — this is real estate, not SaaS

**References:**
- keyproperties.com — professional, understated luxury, serious tone, information clarity
- Boutique real estate firms that feel curated, not like a catalog

### Color Direction

The current purple (#3D1F6B) and gold (#E8B86D) palette is open to evolution. The new palette should:
- Convey sophistication and trust without feeling corporate or cold
- Work in light mode with strong contrast
- Support a warm, inviting feeling (properties are homes, not products)
- Have a distinctive accent that doesn't read as generic "luxury gold"

### Typography Direction

Fonts must feel distinctive and refined. Avoid the AI monoculture (Inter, DM Sans, Cormorant Garamond are explicitly banned by the impeccable skill guidelines). Seek fonts that feel:
- **Display/headings:** Confident, elegant, with personality — like a curated magazine masthead
- **Body:** Highly readable, warm, professional — invites extended reading of property descriptions

### Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every design choice should make information easier to find and understand. Property details, prices, and contact actions must be immediately scannable.
2. **Curated, not crowded** — The site is a boutique vitrina, not a warehouse. Each property gets the presentation it deserves. Whitespace is a feature.
3. **Trust through craft** — Meticulous attention to detail (alignment, spacing, typography) signals professionalism. If the website looks polished, the brokerage must be polished.
4. **Warmth with sophistication** — The interface should feel human and inviting, not sterile. Properties are homes — the design should make you want to live there.
5. **Action-oriented** — Every page should have a clear next step: view the property, call the agent, send a WhatsApp. Reduce friction between interest and action.

### Accessibility
- Intuitive navigation — users should never feel lost
- Clear visual hierarchy — most important information is immediately visible
- Touch-friendly targets for mobile
- Sufficient contrast ratios (WCAG AA minimum)
- Fast loading — property images optimized, lazy loaded
